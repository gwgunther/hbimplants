# Keyword Database — Huntington Beach Prosthodontics

## How to Use This Document

- **Persona** — which patient archetype this keyword attracts (see `personas.md`)
- **Intent** — where the searcher is in their decision process (Research / Compare / Ready)
- **Match type** — Exact [E], Phrase [P], or Broad Modified [B] (start with E + P only)
- **Landing page** — which `/lp/` page to use as the Final URL in Google Ads
- **Priority** — High (start here), Medium (add after 30+ conversions), Low (future expansion)
